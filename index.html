Zhang Xingtian, also known as Kusa, living in Beijing, is software engineer, independent developer, and art & design amateur.

Kusa is rose-colored, and smells kusai.

Contact by Twitter, Instagram or email.

Software Engineer

Kusa works at Douban as a product development engineer who develop web and app backend with Python.

Independent Developer

Kusa used to be an Apple developer who designed and programmed (Swift) experimental applications: Pink Pill, a random poem generator; Encore, a music making and sharing platform.

Pink Pill has a web demake which can be tried here.

Art & Design Amateur

Kusa has been working on contemporary poetry, digital art and graphic design. He has self-published several volumes of poetry in different forms; the latest work is import poetry, poems written in programming language.

He designs logos and icons for his university and friends.

He writes smelly essays and flash fictions. His fiction, The Quizzer, has been published on Gamecores.
